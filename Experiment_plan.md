## Train-test split

The current datasets have been splitted by person, first 80% in training, last 20% in test. This train test split can be used for now for model replication and verification. However, in later study, the ultimate plan can be:

1. Keep a big training dataset
2. Seperate several paticipants specifically for test. No data use for training. 
3. Given the individual heterogenouity, I also want to use a small sample for individual finetuning.
